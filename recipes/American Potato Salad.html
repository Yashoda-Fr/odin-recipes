American Potato Salad

American Potato Salad is a classic side dish in American cuisine. It consists of boiled potatoes that are typically mixed with mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, and various seasonings. Additional ingredients such as chopped onions, celery, pickles, and hard-boiled eggs are often added for extra flavor and texture. The salad is served chilled and is commonly enjoyed at picnics, barbecues, and summer gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds red potatoes
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 cups mayonnaise
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 3 stalks celery, thinly sliced
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add potatoes, and cook for 15 to 20 minutes, or until tender but still firm. Drain, cool and cut into cubes.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add potatoes, and cook for 15 to 20 minutes, or until tender but still firm. Drain, cool and cut into cubes.
  3. In a large bowl, combine chopped potatoes and eggs. Mix together mayonnaise, chopped onion, green onion, green pepper, and celery. Season with salt and pepper, then mix well. Cover, and refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
  4. Enjoy!
